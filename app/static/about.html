About
The Problem

AI moves fast. Keeping up shouldn't be a full-time job.

If you work in AI, you know the feeling. New papers on arXiv every day. Blog posts from OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta, and Hugging Face landing at random hours. YouTube channels breaking down the latest breakthroughs. Important launches buried under hype.

The information is everywhere — scattered across dozens of sources, buried in feeds, and mixed in with noise. By the time you catch up, there's already something new you've missed. It's exhausting, and for professionals who need to stay sharp, it's unsustainable.

The Solution

Lumin: your AI news, filtered and delivered.

Lumin is an AI-powered news aggregation service that scrapes the most important sources in the AI world, summarizes them using LLMs, and delivers a single, personalized daily email digest — ranked by what matters to you.

No accounts to manage. No apps to install. Just choose your domains, enter your email, and wake up to the news that matters.

How It Works

From sources to your inbox, fully automated.

  • Scrapes 6+ sources daily — YouTube (AI channels), OpenAI, Anthropic, Hugging Face, Meta AI, and arXiv
  • LLM-generated summaries for every article, so you get the gist in seconds
  • Personalized curation — articles scored and ranked based on your interests
  • Beautiful email digest with relevance scores, delivered to your inbox every morning
  • Magic-link preferences — update your interests anytime, no login required
  • Powered by Groq (Llama 3.3 70B) for fast, free inference at scale
